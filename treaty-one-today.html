Modern Treaty One

Today, Treaty One Nations are actively engaged in various initiatives to revitalize their culture, language, and traditions. This includes the development of community programs, educational resources, and partnerships with other organizations.

One notable project is the Kapyong Barracks redevelopment, which aims to transform the former military site into a vibrant community hub that reflects the history and culture of the Treaty One Nations.
